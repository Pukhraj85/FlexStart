About Us

VahanSetu is a digital platform which mainly focuses on vehicle privacy & safety needs. The fate of your vehicle after parking and leaving it is uncertain. Life often presents scenarios where alternate outcomes are desired. In times of unforeseen events, the notion of finding an alternative path emerges.
